Registration

A customer will have to first create an account with the application.

Login

Customer will use the details they used for signing up to always log into the application without signing up over again.

Select categories

User can select any of the desired categories of the products available.

Choose options

User can either choose to either order for the desired product at their door or find shop with the desired product.

Placing an order

User is required to enter their details to confirm area of delivery and to choose mathod of payment.

Confirming payment

A form in form of a receipt will be issued to confirm the users payment.

An email will also be sent to the user's gmail account to confirm the payment made.

Finding shop

User starts to move and will get notified when in vicinity of a shop with the desired product.
