In the past years and up to date, people find difficulty in purchasing different items and products of need due to lack of awareness on wheir exactly to purchase them. We have seen several people trecking around town, door to door asking if a particular product is available or not. This is a very tiresome act given that, at times, the day will break and one has not purchased what they wanted.

What is shopify?

Shopify is a country wide marketplace, where people can make; sells, buy and help users to locate shops around their current locations with the items they want.

There is no Shopify warehouse! its just a collection of shops selling products that are on demand by people.

Project objectives

Shopify takes in the description of a product from a user and notifies the the user once they are in vicinity of the described product.

The user will be able to order the product online as well as make payments.

It eases the location of the product which saves the user's time.

What are the benefits of shopify?

1.users will easily locate shops with the items and products they need.

2.Easy order management.

3.Easy access to shops.

Functionalities

    verification of the account details.
    Checking several price of the different products.
    sending notification to a particular user once in vicinity of the shop with the desired product.
